# Vision

## Goal

Build a photography planning application that lists candidate dates and times in the Kanto region when sunrise, sunset, moonrise, or moonset visually aligns with Tokyo Skytree or Tokyo Tower.

## Target users

- photographers planning telephoto skyline shots
- hobbyists comparing multiple viewpoints before a trip
- creators who want a searchable archive of theoretical alignment opportunities

## MVP boundary

- manual registration of shooting spots
- fixed landmarks: Tokyo Skytree and Tokyo Tower
- theoretical alignment search for sunrise, sunset, moonrise, and moonset
- candidate ranking by geometric closeness
- map placeholder and result detail pages

## Later phases

- weather overlays
- moon illumination filters
- obstruction-aware scoring
- focal length preview
- notifications and saved searches
